americana

The Americana Group is one of the most successful organizations in the Middle East region. The group is considered one of the largest food manufacturing and distributing companies in the region. The group was founded back in Kuwait in 1964, and introduced the concept of QSR’s (Quick serving Restaurants) in the regional market in 1970. The group with its two divisions operates 1800 restaurants across the region as well as 29 food production sites across the UAE, KSA, Kuwait and Egypt, offering the safest, most reliable and best quality food
